## Parent Communication Hub

The Communication Hub lets coaches connect with parents through a mobile-friendly Progressive Web App (PWA). Parents receive an invitation, create an account, and access team information from their phone.

### For Coaches

Navigate to **Communication** from your team page to access:

- **[Messages](/guide/communication/messages)** - Direct messaging with parents, view conversations and unread counts
- **[Schedule](/guide/communication/calendar)** - Create team events that parents can RSVP to; your games from the schedule automatically appear in the parent calendar
- **[Videos](/guide/communication/videos)** - Share game film with the whole team or individual players
- **[Reports](/guide/communication/reports)** - Create AI-powered game summaries and player reports with real stats from film analysis
- **[Parents](/guide/communication/parents)** - Invite parents, manage access, designate parent champions
- **[Plan](/guide/communication/plans)** - Choose your Communication Hub plan (Rookie, Varsity, All-Conference, All-State)
- **[Settings](/guide/communication/settings)** - Toggle team treats sign-up and parent-to-parent messaging

### For Parents

Parents access the hub via a mobile-friendly app with these tabs:

- **Messages** - Message your coach or other parents, send photos
- **Schedule** - View team events and games, RSVP for your family, sign up for team treats
- **Videos** - Watch shared game film
- **Reports** - View game summaries with stats and player highlights
- **Directory** - See all parents on the team with their linked players
- **More** - Announcements, settings, sign out

### Getting Started

1. Coach subscribes to a Communication Hub plan
2. Coach invites parents via email from the Parents tab
3. Parents click the invitation link and create an account
4. Parents choose notification preferences (email, SMS, or both)
5. Parents access the PWA from their phone's browser or home screen
