## Where to find it

After signing in, tap the **Player Profile** tab at the bottom of the screen. If you haven't created a profile yet, you'll see a prompt to get started. Tap it to begin.

## Step 1 — Basic information

Enter your athlete's first and last name, their graduation year, and optionally upload a photo. The photo is just for your own use — it helps you identify the profile quickly if you have more than one athlete. None of this information is shared publicly.

## Step 2 — Sport and season

Select the sport your child plays. Football is fully supported right now, with other sports coming soon. Then choose the current season year. This helps organize clips and reports by season so you can look back at previous years later on.

## Step 3 — Success screen and join code

After saving, you'll see a success screen. Here you'll have the option to enter a **join code** — a 6-character code your coach can give you from their roster page.

The join code is how the app connects your profile to the correct player on your coach's roster. Once linked, your account gets access to team features like the schedule, messages from the coach, shared videos, and any clips or reports your coach publishes for your child.

If you don't have a join code yet, you can skip this step. Your profile will still be created, and clips and reports will begin appearing once your coach sends you an invitation or you enter the code later.

## Your profile belongs to you

The athlete profile belongs to your parent account — not to the team and not to the coach. If your child changes teams, moves up a level, or starts a new season, the profile and its history stay with you. The coach has access to relevant information on their end, but they can't delete or alter your profile.
