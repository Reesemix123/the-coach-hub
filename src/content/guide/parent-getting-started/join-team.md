## Two ways to connect to a team

Joining a team is what links your athlete's profile to your coach's roster and unlocks the full parent experience. There are two ways to do it.

**Option 1 — Coach invitation:** Your coach sends an invitation email directly to you. When you click the link in that email and create your account, your profile is automatically connected to the team. Nothing extra to do on your end.

**Option 2 — Join code:** During athlete profile creation, there's a field where you can enter a 6-character join code. Your coach can find this code on their roster page and share it with you — by text, email, or at practice. Just enter the code during setup and the connection is made right away.

Both options do the same thing: they link your athlete to the correct player slot on the team roster, so clips and reports get routed to the right family.

## What unlocks after joining

Once you're connected to a team, the bottom tabs of the app become active:

- **Schedule** — View upcoming games and practices your coach has added to the calendar.
- **Messages** — Read announcements and updates sent by the coaching staff.
- **Videos** — Watch team videos your coach has chosen to share with the roster.
- **Reports** — Read your child's individual game performance reports.
- **Directory** — See other team families and staff contacts (if your coach has enabled this).

If you were already using the app before joining a team, these tabs may have appeared grayed out or locked. They'll fully activate once the team connection is confirmed.

## Not seeing a tab you expect?

Some features depend on your coach setting them up. If the Messages tab is empty or a tab isn't showing content yet, check in with your coach — they may still be getting things set up for the season.
