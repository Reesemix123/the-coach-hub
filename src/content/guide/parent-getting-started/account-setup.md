## Two ways to get started

There are two paths to creating your account, and both work great.

**Option 1 — Coach invitation:** Your coach sends you an email with a sign-up link. Clicking that link brings you directly to the registration page, and your account will be pre-connected to the team once you finish signing up.

**Option 2 — Sign up directly:** Visit the Youth Coach Hub website and create an account on your own. During athlete profile setup, you'll have the option to enter a join code your coach can give you. This links your profile to the team just like the invitation would.

If you're not sure which path to take, ask your coach. Many coaches send invitations at the start of the season, so check your inbox (and your spam folder — they sometimes end up there).

## Walking through the sign-up form

Whether you clicked an invitation link or signed up on your own, the registration form is the same:

1. Enter your first name and last name.
2. Enter your mobile phone number (used for SMS notifications if you choose to enable them).
3. Create a password.
4. Set your notification preferences — you can choose email, SMS text messages, or both. You can change these at any time in your account settings.
5. Accept the terms of service and tap **Create Account**.

That's it. You'll land on the home screen, where you can create your athlete's profile and connect to your team.

## Installing the app on your phone

Youth Coach Hub works as a progressive web app, which means you can add it to your phone's home screen and use it just like a native app — no app store required. For step-by-step instructions on installing it on iPhone or Android, see the **Installing the App** guide.

## Signing in on multiple devices

You can use Youth Coach Hub on your phone, tablet, and computer. Just sign in with the same email address and password on each device. Your athlete's clips, reports, and team info will be available on all of them.
