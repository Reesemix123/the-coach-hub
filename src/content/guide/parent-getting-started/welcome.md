## A home base for you and your athlete

Youth Coach Hub is a platform built for football coaches — and now, for the families behind their players. As a parent, you get your own space to stay connected to your child's season without having to chase down updates or sift through group texts.

From your parent account, you can watch game film clips of your child, read AI-generated performance reports written just for families, check the team schedule, view messages from the coaching staff, and browse team videos your coach has chosen to share.

## Your coach is in control of what gets shared

Coaches use Youth Coach Hub to tag plays, review film, and build out their playbooks. The clips and reports you see are a byproduct of that work — and they only appear after your coach has reviewed and approved them for sharing.

That means you won't see raw or unreviewed footage. Everything that comes through to your account has been intentionally shared by the coaching staff. Think of it less like a live camera feed and more like a curated highlight reel, delivered right after film review is complete.

## Clips and reports tied to your child

The platform connects to your athlete's profile, so everything you see is specific to your child — not a generic team feed. Clips show the plays where your child was directly involved. Reports are written about their individual performance in each game.

If your child plays on multiple teams or moves from one season to the next, their profile travels with them. You won't lose past highlights or reports when a new season starts.

## Getting started is simple

All you need is an email address and either an invitation from your coach or a join code. The next few pages walk you through account setup, creating your athlete's profile, and joining your team. It takes about five minutes to get everything connected.
