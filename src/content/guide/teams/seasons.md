## Overview

The Schedule page is your central hub for managing games, practices, and team events throughout the season. It features a calendar view that helps you visualize your entire season at a glance.

## Accessing the Schedule

1. Navigate to your team dashboard
2. Click **Schedule** in the team navigation bar
3. You'll see the calendar view with your games and events

## Calendar View

The schedule displays a monthly calendar showing:

- **Games** - Displayed with opponent name and score (if played)
- **Practices** - Team practice sessions
- **Events** - Meetings, film sessions, and other activities

### Navigating the Calendar

- Use the **arrow buttons** to move between months
- Click on any date to see details for that day
- Games are highlighted differently than practices and events

### Season Record

At the top of the schedule, you'll see your team's current record:

- **Wins** - Games marked as wins
- **Losses** - Games marked as losses
- **Ties** - Games marked as ties

## Adding Games

### Create a New Game

1. Click the **Add** button
2. Select **Game**
3. Fill in the game details:

| Field | Description | Required |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **Game Name** | A name for the game (e.g., "Week 1") | Yes |
| **Opponent** | Name of the opposing team | Yes |
| **Date** | Game date | Yes |
| **Start Time** | Kickoff time | No |
| **Location** | Field or stadium name | No |
| **Notes** | Any additional information | No |

4. Click **Save**

### Recording Game Results

After a game is played, update it with the results:

1. Click on the game in the calendar
2. Click **Edit**
3. Fill in:
   - **Game Result** - Win, Loss, or Tie
   - **Team Score** - Your team's final score
   - **Opponent Score** - Opponent's final score
   - **Quarter Scores** (optional) - Score by quarter for detailed tracking

4. Click **Save**

### Quarter-by-Quarter Scoring

For detailed game tracking, you can enter scores by quarter:

- Q1, Q2, Q3, Q4 scores for both teams
- Overtime score (if applicable)
- These feed into quarter-by-quarter analytics

## Adding Events

### Create a Practice

1. Click the **Add** button
2. Select **Event**
3. Set **Event Type** to "Practice"
4. Fill in details:
   - Title
   - Date and time
   - Location
   - Description

5. Click **Save**

### Create Other Events

Use events for:

- **Practice** - Regular practice sessions
- **Meeting** - Team meetings, film review sessions
- **Other** - Any other team activity

## Opponent Games (Scouting)

You can add opponent games for scouting purposes:

1. When adding a game, check **"This is an opponent's game (for scouting)"**
2. Enter the opponent's team name
3. This game will be flagged for scouting analysis
4. Upload opponent film to this game for defensive preparation

## Linking to Film

Games you create appear in your Film section. When you upload game video:

1. Navigate to **Film**
2. Select the game from your schedule
3. Upload video files
4. Start tagging plays

> **Tip:** Create games in your schedule before uploading film. This keeps everything organized.

## Linking to Practice Plans

Practice sessions can be linked to your practice plans:

1. Create a practice plan in the **Practice** section
2. Assign it to a specific date
3. The practice appears on your schedule calendar
4. Click to view or edit the practice plan

## Managing Your Season

### Setting Up a New Season

At the start of a season:

1. Add all scheduled games
2. Create recurring practice events
3. Add important dates (first day of practice, playoff dates, etc.)

### Mid-Season Updates

Keep your schedule current by:

- Updating game results after each game
- Adjusting rescheduled games
- Adding playoff games as they're scheduled

### End of Season

After the season:

- Ensure all game results are recorded
- Your complete season record is preserved
- Analytics reflect the full season of data

## Calendar Tips

### Color Coding

- **Games** are typically shown in a distinct color
- **Wins** may show in green, **Losses** in red
- **Upcoming games** vs **Past games** are distinguished

### Quick Actions

Click on any calendar item to:

- View details
- Edit the item
- Navigate to related film (for games)
- Access the practice plan (for practices)

### Filtering

Use filters to show:

- Games only
- Practices only
- All items

## Best Practices

### Name Games Consistently

Use a consistent format like:

- "Week 1 vs Eastside"
- "vs. Westside Tigers (Homecoming)"
- "Playoff - Round 1"

### Add Games Early

Enter your schedule at the start of the season:

- Easier to plan ahead
- Ready for film uploads after each game
- Coaches can see the full schedule

### Update Results Promptly

After each game:

1. Record the final score
2. Add quarter scores if tracking that level of detail
3. Upload game film within a few days

### Use Events for Non-Game Activities

Track everything on one calendar:

- Parent meetings
- Equipment pickup
- Film review sessions
- Weight room sessions

## Troubleshooting

### Game Not Showing on Calendar?

- Check that the date is set correctly
- Navigate to the correct month
- Verify the game was saved

### Can't Edit a Game?

- Only coaches and owners can edit games
- Check your role in Team Settings

### Score Mismatch Warning?

If the quarter scores don't add up to the final score, you'll see a warning. Update either:

- The quarter-by-quarter scores
- The final score
