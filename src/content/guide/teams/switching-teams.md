# Switching Teams

If you coach multiple teams, Youth Coach Hub makes it easy to switch between them.

## From the Dashboard

Your dashboard shows a card for each team you own or belong to. Each card displays:

- Team name
- Sport (Football)
- Level badge (Youth, JV, Varsity)

Tap **Open** on any team card to go directly to that team's workspace.

If you have more than one team, a **View all teams** link appears at the top right of the "Your Teams" section.

## From the Top Navigation Bar

When you're inside any team page, a **team switcher pill** appears in the top navigation bar next to the logo. It shows your current team name.

1. Click the team name pill
2. A dropdown appears listing all your teams
3. The active team has a checkmark
4. Click any team to switch — you'll navigate to that team's dashboard

## On Mobile

On mobile screens, tap your **avatar** (the circle in the top right of the header) to open a bottom sheet:

1. All your teams are listed with level badges
2. The active team has a lime green checkmark
3. Tap a team to switch — the playbook, roster, and sideline screens update immediately
4. **Account Settings** link at the bottom

## Tips

- Your mobile team selection is remembered between sessions
- All screens (Playbook, Roster, Sideline, Home) automatically use the active team's data
- On desktop, the team context comes from the URL — each team has its own `/football/teams/[teamId]` workspace
