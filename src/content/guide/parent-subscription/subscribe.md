## How to subscribe

Subscribing takes just a few minutes. Here's how to do it:

1. Open your athlete's profile by tapping the Player Profile tab in the bottom navigation.
2. Scroll to the bottom of the page to find the subscription card. It will show a summary of how many clips and reports are saved to your athlete's profile.
3. Tap the button labeled **Subscribe — $19.99/year**.
4. You'll be taken to a secure checkout page powered by Stripe, the same payment processor used by millions of apps and websites.
5. Enter your payment information and confirm the purchase.
6. You'll be redirected back to your athlete's profile, where full access will be active immediately.

## What happens after you subscribe

Your subscription is active from the moment the payment is confirmed. Any content that was previously locked — past season clips, reports, and stats — becomes accessible right away.

Your subscription will automatically renew each year on the same date. You'll receive a reminder email 7 days before each renewal so you're never caught off guard. If you decide you no longer want to renew, you can cancel before the renewal date and your access will continue through the end of the current billing period.

## Managing your subscription

To view your billing details, update your payment method, or cancel your subscription, visit the billing portal. You can find a link to the portal in your account settings. The billing portal is also managed through Stripe and is accessible from any browser.

## Accepted payment methods

Coach Hub accepts all major credit and debit cards through Stripe's secure checkout. This includes Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover.

## Questions or issues

If you run into any trouble during checkout or have a question about your subscription, reach out to us through the Help section in the app. We're happy to assist.
