## When Your Subscription Ends

Here's a clear picture of what changes and what stays the same.

**Current season content** — if the team's Communication Hub plan is still active, you keep free access to current season content. Nothing changes there.

**Past seasons** — clips and reports from previous seasons will show a lock icon. You can still see the event card (opponent, date, and performance grade) but the full report and video clip will be locked until you resubscribe.

## The 90-Day Grace Period

After your subscription lapses, your data is held for 90 days. During this window, your athlete's profile page shows a countdown so you always know how much time is left.

If you resubscribe within those 90 days, everything unlocks immediately. No data is lost.

## After 90 Days

Once the grace period ends, your data is archived and moved to long-term storage. It is not permanently deleted.

If you need to restore archived data, reach out to support and the team can assist you.

## Avoiding Interruption

The easiest way to keep uninterrupted access is to either keep your subscription active or resubscribe before the 90-day grace period ends. Check your renewal reminder email (sent 7 days before your charge date) to make sure your payment method is current.
