## Accessing Your Billing Portal

From your athlete's profile page, find the subscription card and tap the billing management link. This opens a secure portal where you can manage everything related to your subscription.

From the billing portal you can:

- **Update your payment method** — add a new card or replace an expired one
- **View past invoices and receipts** — download PDFs for your records
- **Cancel your subscription** — stop future charges at any time

## About Cancellation

If you cancel, your access continues through the end of your current billing period. Since subscriptions are billed annually, you'll keep full access for the remainder of the year you've already paid for.

Once that period ends, past season clips and reports will be locked. You can still see your athlete's profile, but the content won't be viewable until you resubscribe.

Resubscribing at any time will unlock everything immediately.

## Renewal Reminders

You'll receive an email 7 days before your next charge so there are no surprises. If your payment method has changed or expired, that's a good time to update it in the billing portal.
