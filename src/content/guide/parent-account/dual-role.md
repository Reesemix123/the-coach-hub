## One Account, Two Views

If you're both a coach and a parent of a player, you don't need separate accounts. Youth Coach Hub lets you switch between your coach dashboard and your parent view at any time.

## How It Works

Your single login gives you access to both:

- **Coach view** — your full coaching dashboard with playbooks, film analysis, analytics, and team management
- **Parent view** — the parent app with your athlete's clips, reports, schedule, and team communication

Both views are always available. You're never locked into one or the other.

## Switching to Parent View

From the coach dashboard:

1. Tap your **avatar** (the circle with your initial) in the top-right corner
2. Select **Switch to parent view**
3. You'll be taken directly to the parent app

You can also use the **Parent view card** that appears on your dashboard — tap **Switch →** to jump to the parent app.

## Switching to Coach View

From the parent app:

1. Tap **More** in the bottom navigation
2. Tap **Switch to coach view** (shown in green at the top of the menu)
3. You'll be taken back to your coaching dashboard

## Setting Up Both Roles

If you're already a coach and want to add a parent profile:

1. From the coach dashboard, tap your avatar and select **Switch to parent view**
2. Tap the **Player Profile** tab at the bottom
3. Follow the steps to create your athlete's profile

If you purchased a Communication Hub plan, you may also see a prompt asking "Do you have a child on this team?" — tap **Set up parent profile** to get started.

## Good to Know

- Your coaching data and parent data are completely separate — switching views doesn't affect either one
- The AI Assistant adapts to your current view (coaching help on the dashboard, parent help in the parent app)
- You can switch back and forth as many times as you want
