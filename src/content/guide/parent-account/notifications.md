## Types of Notifications

You can receive notifications for the following events:

- New game clips available for your athlete
- New performance reports published
- Team announcements from your coaching staff
- Schedule changes and newly added events
- Subscription renewal reminders (sent 7 days before your charge date)

## How to Update Your Preferences

Tap **More** in the bottom navigation, then tap **Settings**. From there you can choose to receive notifications by email, SMS, or both. Changes take effect immediately and you can update them at any time.

## SMS Notifications

If you opted in to SMS during signup, you'll receive text messages for time-sensitive updates like new clips and schedule changes.

To opt out of SMS at any time, either reply **STOP** to any message or turn off SMS in your notification settings.

## Email Notifications

Emails are sent to the address you used when creating your account. If you'd like to use a different email address, update it in Settings under your account profile.
