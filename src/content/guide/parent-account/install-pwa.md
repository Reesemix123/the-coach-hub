## What Is the App?

Youth Coach Hub works as a web app that you can install on your phone's home screen. Once installed, it looks and feels like a regular app — with its own icon, full-screen experience, and faster loading.

It's not available in the App Store or Google Play. Instead, you add it directly from your phone's web browser.

## Installing on iPhone (Safari)

1. Open **Safari** and go to [youthcoachhub.com](https://youthcoachhub.com)
2. Sign in to your parent account
3. Tap the **Share** button (the square with an arrow pointing up) at the bottom of the screen
4. Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**
5. You'll see "Youth Coach Hub" as the app name — tap **Add**
6. The app icon will appear on your home screen

> **Important:** You must use Safari for this to work. Chrome and other browsers on iPhone don't support home screen installation.

## Installing on Android (Chrome)

1. Open **Chrome** and go to [youthcoachhub.com](https://youthcoachhub.com)
2. Sign in to your parent account
3. Tap the **three-dot menu** (⋮) in the top-right corner
4. Tap **Add to Home screen** (or **Install app** if shown)
5. Confirm by tapping **Add**
6. The app icon will appear on your home screen or app drawer

## After Installing

Once installed:

- Tap the **Youth Coach Hub** icon on your home screen to open the app
- The app opens in full screen without browser controls
- It loads faster than visiting the website
- Notifications work better from the installed version

## Troubleshooting

### Don't see "Add to Home Screen"?

- **iPhone**: Make sure you're using Safari, not Chrome or another browser
- **Android**: Make sure you're using Chrome. Try refreshing the page first
- Make sure you're signed in before installing

### App isn't updating?

The app updates automatically when you open it. If something looks outdated, close the app completely and reopen it.

### Want to uninstall?

- **iPhone**: Press and hold the app icon, then tap **Remove App**
- **Android**: Press and hold the icon, then drag to **Uninstall** or tap the X
