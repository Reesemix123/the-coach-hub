## Offense

**Run Game**
- "How is my run game?"
- "What's our yards per carry?"
- "Which formations work best for running?"

**Pass Game**
- "How is my passing attack?"
- "What's our completion percentage?"
- "Pass game on third down?"

**Offensive Line**
- "How is my offensive line doing?"
- "Who's my best blocker?"
- "How is our pass protection?"

---

## Defense

**Tackles**
- "Who's my leading tackler?"
- "Show me tackle leaders"

**Pass Rush**
- "How is our pass rush?"
- "Who's getting sacks?"
- "How many pressures do we have?"

**Playmakers**
- "Who's making plays on defense?"
- "Show me defensive stats"

---

## Special Teams

**Kicking**
- "How is my kicker doing?"
- "What's our field goal percentage?"
- "How are we on PATs?"

**Punting**
- "How is my punter?"
- "What's our punt average?"

**Returns**
- "Who's my best return man?"
- "How is our kick return game?"
- "Punt return stats?"

**Coverage**
- "How is our punt coverage?"
- "How is my long snapper?"

---

## Situations

**Third Down**
- "How are we on third down?"
- "Third down conversion rate?"

**Red Zone**
- "Red zone efficiency?"
- "How do we do in the red zone?"

**First Down**
- "First down tendencies?"
- "What do we run on first down?"

---

## Players

**By Number**
- "How is #22 doing?"
- "Stats for #7"

**By Name**
- "How is Marcus doing?"
- "Stats for Johnson"

**By Position**
- "How are my running backs?"
- "Linebacker stats?"

## Trends
