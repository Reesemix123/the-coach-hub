## Overview

The Special Teams Report covers all phases of the kicking game: kickoffs, punts, field goals, extra points, and returns. Track hidden yardage and field position impact.

## Accessing the Report

1. Navigate to **Analytics** in your team dashboard
2. Select **Special Teams Report** from the report selector
3. Use filters to analyze all games or specific games

## Using the Report

### Evaluate Hidden Yardage

Special teams often decide close games:

- Are you winning the field position battle?
- How does net punting compare?
- Are returns helping or hurting?

### Identify Weaknesses

Look for problem areas:

- Kickoff coverage issues
- Punt return struggles
- Field goal range limitations

### Track Development

Monitor kicker/punter progress:

- Distance improvements
- Accuracy trends
- Consistency patterns

### Game Planning

Use special teams data for:

- Return decisions (when to return vs. fair catch)
- Field goal range expectations
- Kickoff strategies
