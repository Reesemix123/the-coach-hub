## Data-Driven Coaching

Analytics in Youth Coach Hub transforms your tagged film into actionable insights. Track team performance, identify trends over the season, and evaluate individual player development.

### What You'll Learn

- **[Team Analytics](/guide/analytics/team-analytics)** - Overall team performance metrics and play effectiveness
- **[Season Trends](/guide/analytics/season-trends)** - Track improvement and patterns across games
- **[Player Stats](/guide/analytics/player-stats)** - Individual player performance and development

### Prerequisites

To generate meaningful analytics, you need:

1. **Tagged Film** - Plays tagged with outcomes (yards, success, etc.)
2. **Roster Setup** - Players added to track individual stats
3. **Multiple Games** - More data = better insights

### Analytics Philosophy

Youth Coach Hub analytics focus on:

- **Success Rate** - Did the play achieve its goal?
- **Efficiency** - Yards per play, points per drive
- **Situational Performance** - How you perform in key situations
- **Development** - Are players and schemes improving?

### Quick Start

1. [Tag at least one game](/guide/film/tagging-plays) with play outcomes
2. Navigate to **Analytics** in your team dashboard
3. Explore team-wide metrics
4. Drill down into player-specific stats
