## Overview

The Player Report provides individual performance metrics for each player on your roster. Select a player to see their complete stats, game log, and development trends.

## Accessing the Report

1. Navigate to **Analytics** in your team dashboard
2. Select **Player Report** from the report selector
3. **Select a player** from the player dropdown

> **Note:** You must select a player to view this report.

---

## Player Selection

### By Position Group

Players are organized by position:

- **Quarterbacks** - QB stats
- **Running Backs** - RB/FB stats
- **Receivers** - WR/TE stats
- **Offensive Line** - OL stats (LT, LG, C, RG, RT)
- **Defensive Line** - DL stats
- **Linebackers** - LB stats
- **Defensive Backs** - DB stats (CB, S)
- **Special Teams** - K, P, returners

## Tips

### Accurate Attribution

Player stats depend on correct tagging:

- Attribute plays to right players
- Verify jersey numbers
- Double-check unusual stats

### Context Matters

Remember stats don't tell everything:

- Opponent quality
- Blocking/coverage help
- Scheme responsibilities
