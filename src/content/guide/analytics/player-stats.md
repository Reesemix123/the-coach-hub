## Overview

Player Stats provides individual performance metrics for every player on your roster. Track development over the season, compare players at the same position, and identify your top performers in key categories.

## Accessing Player Stats

### From Analytics

1. Navigate to **Analytics** in your team dashboard
2. Select the **Players** tab
3. View leaderboards and player rankings

### From Player Profile

1. Navigate to **Players** in your team dashboard
2. Click on a specific player
3. View their individual stat page

## Prerequisites

To generate player stats, you need:

1. **Roster Setup** - Players added with positions
2. **Tagged Film** - Plays tagged with player attribution
3. **Tagging Level** - Standard or Comprehensive tagging captures more player data

## Best Practices

### Accurate Attribution

For reliable stats:

- Attribute plays to correct players
- Verify jersey numbers
- Double-check unusual stats

### Context Matters

Remember stats don't tell everything:

- Opponent quality
- Blocking/coverage help
- Scheme responsibilities
- Role within the team

### Consistent Tracking

For meaningful comparisons:

- Tag every game
- Use same tagging depth
- Attribute all players involved

### Celebrate Improvement

Focus on development:

- Personal bests
- Improvement trends
- Effort indicators

## Troubleshooting

### Stats Not Showing

If player stats are missing:

- Verify player is on roster
- Check that plays are attributed to them
- Ensure correct tagging level used

### Incorrect Stats

If numbers seem wrong:

- Review tagged plays
- Check for duplicate entries
- Verify player attribution

### Missing Players

If players aren't appearing:

- Add them to the roster
- Tag new plays with their attribution
- Check position filters
