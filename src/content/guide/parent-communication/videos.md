## Two Types of Video Content

### Team Videos

These are shared by the coach to all parents on the team. Find them in the **Videos** tab at the bottom of the screen.

Team videos might include game highlights, announcements, or practice clips that the coaching staff wants everyone to see.

### Athlete Clips

These are specific to your child's performance. Find them on your athlete's profile page under **Highlights**.

Athlete clips are automatically generated from game film analysis — they're pulled from the footage where your child was involved in the play.

## How Playback Works

Videos stream directly in the app. No download is required, and you don't need to install anything extra.

Playback uses secure streaming, which means videos cannot be saved or redistributed outside the app.

## If a Video Shows "Processing"

Game film takes a few minutes to process after it's uploaded. If you see a "Processing" message on a clip, check back in a few minutes and it should be ready.
