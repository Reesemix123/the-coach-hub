## Where to Find It

Tap the **Schedule** tab at the bottom of the screen. This shows everything on the team's calendar in one place.

## What You'll See

Each event card shows the date, time, location, and event type. The schedule includes:

- Games
- Practices
- Team meetings
- Other team activities

Games display the opponent's name and are marked with a trophy icon. Once a game is complete, the card updates to show the final score and result (Win, Loss, or Tie).

## Getting Directions

Tap the location on any event card to open it directly in your phone's maps app.

## RSVP

Some events have an RSVP option. When one is available, you'll see response buttons right on the event card. See the RSVP guide for full details on how to respond and update your answer.
