## How to RSVP

When an event has RSVP enabled, you'll see response buttons directly on the event card in the **Schedule** tab.

Your options are:

- **Attending**
- **Maybe**
- **Not Attending**

Tap your choice and it's recorded right away. No extra steps needed.

## Changing Your Response

You can update your RSVP at any time before the event — just tap a different option on the event card. Your updated response saves immediately.

## Families With Multiple Children on the Team

If you have more than one child on the roster, you'll see a family RSVP option that lets you set a separate response for each athlete. That way coaches know exactly who to expect.

## Why RSVP Matters

Your coach uses attendance counts to plan transportation, equipment, and team activities. Even a "Maybe" is helpful. An accurate headcount means your coach can prepare a better experience for everyone on the team.
