## Where to Find Messages

Tap the **Messages** tab at the bottom of the screen to open your conversations.

## Starting a Conversation

Tap the **New Message** button to compose a message to your coaching staff. Messages are sent to all coaches on the team, not just one individual. This ensures nothing slips through the cracks if a coach is unavailable.

## Reading Replies

When a coach responds, you'll see it in your conversation thread. Depending on your notification preferences, you may also receive an alert via email or SMS. You can adjust those settings anytime under More > Settings.

## Parent-to-Parent Messaging

If your coach has enabled this feature, you can also message other parents through the **Directory** tab. This is great for coordinating rides, snacks, or team activities.

## A Note About Response Times

Many coaches are volunteers who are balancing practice, games, and their own family obligations. Please allow a reasonable amount of time for a response, especially during busy game weeks. If something is time-sensitive, note that in your message.
