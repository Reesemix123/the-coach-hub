## Choose Your Plan

Youth Coach Hub offers flexible subscription plans designed for teams of all sizes and needs. From basic film storage to advanced analytics and AI features, there's a plan that fits your program.

### What You'll Learn

- **[Plan Comparison](/guide/subscriptions/tier-comparison)** - Compare features across all plans
- **[Upgrading Your Plan](/guide/subscriptions/upgrading)** - How to upgrade or downgrade
- **[Billing & Payments](/guide/subscriptions/billing)** - Manage payments and invoices

### Plan Overview

| Plan | Best For | Key Features |
|------|----------|--------------|
| **Basic** | Getting started | 2 uploads/month, essential features |
| **Plus** | Active programs | 4 uploads/month, play tagging, analytics |
| **Premium** | Serious teams | 8 uploads/month, advanced analytics, AI features |

### Quick Comparison

| Feature | Basic | Plus | Premium |
|---------|-------|------|---------|
| Monthly Uploads | 2 | 4 | 8 |
| Camera Angles | 1 | 3 | 5 |
| Play Tagging | Basic | Full | Full + AI assist |
| Analytics | Basic | Standard | Advanced |
| Coaches | 3 | 10 | Unlimited |
| Storage | 30 days | 90 days | 1 year |
