### Plan Overview

| Plan | Best For | Monthly Price |
|------|----------|---------------|
| **Basic** | Getting started | Free |
| **Plus** | Active programs | $29.99/month |
| **Premium** | Serious teams | $79.99/month |

### Quick Comparison

| Capacity | Basic | Plus | Premium |
|----------|-------|------|---------|
| Team Games/Month | 1 | 2 | 4 |
| Opponent Games/Month | 1 | 2 | 4 |
| Cameras per Game | 1 | 3 | 5 |
| Storage per Game | 5 GB | 10 GB | 25 GB |
| Video Retention | 30 days | 180 days | 365 days |
| Coaches | 3 | 5 | 10 |

**All plans include full access to:** Playbook builder, all tagging levels, full analytics suite, player statistics, game planning tools, and more.
