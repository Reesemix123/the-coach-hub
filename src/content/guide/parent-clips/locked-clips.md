## Two reasons a clip might be locked

If you see a lock icon on a clip, there are two possible explanations.

**Pending review** — Your coach hasn't approved this clip yet. Before any clip becomes visible to families, coaches review it to make sure the content is accurate and appropriate to share. This is a deliberate step — coaches want to make sure what you see is meaningful and correct, not just raw unfiltered footage. Most clips are approved within a day or two of the game, so if you're seeing a lot of lock icons right after game night, give it a little time.

**Season expired** — The team's communication plan for the season has ended, and you don't have an active Player Profile subscription. Once a season wraps up, access to that season's clips requires a subscription to keep your archive available. A **Player Profile subscription is $19.99 per year** and gives you permanent access to all clips and reports from every season. See the **Subscription** guide for details on how to subscribe.

## Just joined and not seeing any clips yet?

If you recently joined the team and your child's highlights section looks empty, that's normal. Clips are generated after your coach completes film analysis for each game — it's not an instant process. Give the coaching staff a day or two after each game, and clips will start to appear.

If it's been more than a week after a game and you still don't see any clips, it's worth reaching out to the coaching staff to make sure your profile is correctly linked to your child's player slot on the roster.
