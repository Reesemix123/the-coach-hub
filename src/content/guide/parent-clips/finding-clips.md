## Where clips live

To find your child's game clips, tap the **Player Profile** tab at the bottom of the screen. Scroll down past the athlete's info to the **Highlights** section. That's where all of their clips will appear.

Clips are organized by game, with the most recent game listed first. If your child has played multiple games and the coach has completed film review for each one, you'll see clips grouped by opponent and date.

## Filtering by unit

Above the clips, you'll see four filter buttons: **All**, **Offense**, **Defense**, and **Special Teams**. Tapping one of these narrows the view to show only clips from that unit.

This is handy if you want to quickly find a specific type of play — like all of your child's defensive snaps from last Friday's game, or just the offensive plays from the first half.

## What each clip card shows

Each clip card displays:
- The opponent's name and the game date
- The play result (for example, "12-yard gain" or "sack")
- A thumbnail preview of the video

If your coach added a note to the clip — some coaches like to add brief observations or encouragement — it will appear directly below the video when you tap to play it.

## Clips appear after film review

Don't be surprised if you don't see any clips right after game night. Clips only show up once your coach has completed film analysis and approved them for sharing. For most coaches, this happens within a day or two of the game. If it's been more than a week and you still don't see clips, it's worth a quick check-in with the coaching staff.
