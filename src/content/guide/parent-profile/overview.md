## Getting to the profile page

Tap the Player Profile tab in the bottom navigation bar. This takes you directly to your athlete's profile. Everything about your child's season — stats, reports, and highlights — lives here.

## The profile header

At the top of the page, you'll see your athlete's profile photo (or their initials if no photo has been added), along with their name, team name, position, jersey number, and graduation year. You'll also see a small badge indicating their subscription status — either showing that the current season is covered by the team's plan, or showing whether an individual Player Profile subscription is active.

## Season stats strip

Just below the header is a row of key stats for the current season — things like games played, average grade, and position-specific numbers. These update as new game reports are published throughout the season. For a full explanation of each stat, see the Understanding Performance Stats guide.

## Game reports

Scrolling down, you'll find your athlete's game reports listed with the most recent first. Each report card shows the opponent, the date, and a quick summary of how your child performed. Tap any report to open the full version, which includes your coach's written notes and detailed stats for that game.

## Highlights

Below the game reports, you'll find the highlights section — video clips selected by your coach from game film. These clips are tied to specific plays and may include a coach's note explaining what they're showing. Highlights are a great way to celebrate good moments and revisit plays your athlete is proud of.

## Subscription card

At the bottom of the profile page, you may see a subscription card. This shows a summary of how much content is saved to your athlete's profile and provides the option to subscribe to a Player Profile plan if you'd like to preserve access to past seasons. If the current season is fully covered by your coach's plan, this card may simply show a status summary rather than a purchase prompt.

## A note about your athlete's data

The profile belongs to your athlete — and to you as their parent. Even if the team changes coaches, your child moves to a different team, or the program ends, the data that has already been collected stays on their profile. You are never starting from scratch.

## Switching between seasons

If your athlete has played more than one season, a dropdown selector will appear near the top of the profile page. Use it to switch between seasons and view the stats, reports, and highlights specific to that year.
