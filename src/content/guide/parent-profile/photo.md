## Why add a photo?

A profile photo makes the athlete profile feel personal and helps coaches and staff quickly identify your child, especially on teams with large rosters. It appears as a circular image in the profile header alongside your athlete's name and details.

## Adding a photo when creating the profile

When you first create your athlete's profile, you'll go through a short setup process. On Step 1, you'll see a circle icon placeholder where the profile photo will go. Tap that circle to open your phone's photo library and choose an image.

## Supported formats and size

The profile photo must be in JPEG, PNG, or WebP format. The maximum file size is 5 MB. Most photos taken on a modern phone will work without any adjustments.

## If you skip the photo

No problem. If you don't add a photo during setup, the profile will display your athlete's initials inside a colored circle instead. This looks clean and works just fine — many families prefer the initials look.

## Changing the photo later

You can add or update the profile photo at any time by opening your athlete's profile and tapping the edit option. From there, you can select a new image from your camera roll. The update takes effect immediately.

## Who can see the photo?

The profile photo is visible to you and to your athlete's coach. It is not shared publicly unless you choose to share the profile link (a feature available with a Player Profile subscription). No photo is ever posted or distributed without your action.
