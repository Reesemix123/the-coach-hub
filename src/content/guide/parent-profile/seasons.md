## How seasons work

Each time your athlete participates in a season with a team using Coach Hub, that season is added to their profile as its own record. Over time, your athlete builds a history that spans multiple years — a timeline of their growth as a player.

## Switching between seasons

At the top of the athlete profile page, you'll see a season selector dropdown if your child has played more than one season. Tap it to switch between years. Each season has its own stats, game reports, and highlights that are separate from every other season.

## What you see per season

When you select a season, everything on the profile — the stats strip, the game reports list, and the highlights section — updates to show only the content from that season. This makes it easy to compare how your athlete performed in their sophomore year versus their junior year, for example.

## Free access during an active season

While your coach's Communication Hub plan is active, you have full access to everything in the current season at no charge. You can view all clips, read all reports, and see all stats without a subscription.

## Accessing past seasons

After a season ends or a coach's plan expires, content from that season becomes locked for free accounts. You'll see a lock icon on clips and reports, but nothing is deleted. Your child's data is preserved and waiting.

A Player Profile subscription ($19.99/year) unlocks all past seasons immediately. If you subscribe after a season has been locked, everything becomes accessible again right away — nothing is lost.

## Your athlete's history is theirs to keep

Even if your athlete switches teams, changes coaches, or takes a year off, their past seasons remain on their profile. The history belongs to your athlete, not to any particular team or program.
