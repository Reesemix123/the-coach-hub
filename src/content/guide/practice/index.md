## Practice Planning

The Practice section helps you plan, organize, and manage your team's practice sessions. Build structured practice plans with periods, drills, and position-specific work, then link them to your schedule.

### What You'll Learn

- **[Creating Practice Plans](/guide/practice/creating-practice-plans)** - Build structured practices with periods and drills
- **[Practice Templates](/guide/practice/templates)** - Save and reuse your best practice formats

### Key Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Practice Plans** | Create detailed plans with timed periods |
| **Period Types** | Warmup, drill, team, special teams, conditioning |
| **Position Groups** | Assign drills to specific position groups |
| **Timeline View** | Visualize your practice schedule |
| **Playbook Integration** | Link plays to drills for focused reps |
| **Templates** | Save and reuse successful practice formats |
| **Schedule Integration** | Practices appear on your team calendar |

### Getting Started

1. Navigate to **Practice** in your team dashboard
2. Click **New Practice Plan**
3. Add periods and drills to build your practice
4. Save and optionally add to your schedule
