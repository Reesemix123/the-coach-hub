## Welcome to Youth Coach Hub

Youth Coach Hub is a comprehensive platform designed to help football coaches at all levels manage their teams, build playbooks, analyze game film, and track performance analytics.

## Quick Setup Checklist

Get your team ready in four simple steps:

1. **Create Your Team** - Set up your team with basic information
2. **Add Your Roster** - Enter player names, positions, and jersey numbers
3. **Upload Game Film** - Get your video footage into the platform
4. **Start Tagging** - Mark plays to unlock analytics

## Core Features

### Team Management

Manage your roster, track playing time, and organize your coaching staff. Each team member can have different access levels based on their role.

### Digital Playbook

Build your playbook directly in the platform. Create formations, draw routes, and organize plays by situation. Share plays with your coaching staff instantly.

### Film Analysis

Upload game footage and tag individual plays with detailed information. The platform tracks everything from basic outcomes to advanced metrics like blocking grades and coverage assignments.

### Analytics Dashboard

Turn your tagged data into actionable insights. See success rates by formation, identify your best plays, track player development, and prepare for opponents.

## Choosing Your Tagging Level

Youth Coach Hub offers different levels of detail based on your team's needs:

| Level | Best For | Key Features |
|-------|----------|--------------|
| Quick | Game recording | Score, yards, turnovers, key moments |
| Standard | Game planning | Play concepts, formations, drive analytics |
| Comprehensive | Player development | Position grades, individual performance metrics |

You can change your tagging level at any time when starting film analysis.
