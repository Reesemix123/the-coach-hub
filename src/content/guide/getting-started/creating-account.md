## Overview

Creating an account is your first step to accessing Youth Coach Hub's coaching tools. The process takes just a few minutes and you'll be ready to set up your team.

## Sign Up Options

There are two ways to create an account:

### 1. New Team Owner

If you're starting a new team or organization:

1. Visit the **Pricing** page and select a plan
2. Click **Get Started** to begin the signup process
3. You'll create your account and then set up your first team

### 2. Invited by Another Coach

If you've received an invitation to join an existing team:

1. Click the invitation link in your email
2. Create your account using the signup form
3. You'll automatically be added to the team that invited you

## Step-by-Step Account Creation

### 1. Enter Your Information

Fill in the signup form with:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Full Name | Your name as you want it displayed to other coaches |
| Email | Your email address (used for login and notifications) |
| Password | At least 8 characters |
| Confirm Password | Re-enter your password to verify |

### 2. Accept Terms of Service

Review and accept the terms of service by checking the checkbox at the bottom of the form.

### 3. Verify Your Email

After submitting the form:

1. Check your email inbox for a confirmation message
2. Click the **Confirm Email** link in the message
3. You'll be redirected to complete your setup

> **Note:** If you don't see the email, check your spam folder. The email comes from Youth Coach Hub.

## After Creating Your Account

Once your account is verified, you'll be directed to:

- **Team Setup** - If you're creating a new team
- **Your Team Dashboard** - If you joined via an invitation

## Troubleshooting

### Didn't receive the confirmation email?

- Check your spam/junk folder
- Make sure you entered the correct email address
- Try signing up again if needed

### Email already in use?

If you see this error, you may already have an account. Try:

1. Using the **Forgot Password** link to reset your password
2. Signing in with the existing account

### Password requirements

Your password must:

- Be at least 8 characters long
- We recommend using a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols
